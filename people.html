Dr. Pramoda Kumar

Principal Investigator | Assistant Professor of Physics

Dr Kumar is Assistant Professor of Physics at Ashoka University and leads the Soft Matter Lab. He completed his PhD from CeNS, Bangalore, and has worked as post-doc at several prestigious institutions including Max Planck, Harvard and Weizmann. His research interests include experimental soft-condensed matter physics, pattern formation in dissipative systems, interface instability, charge transport phenomena in ion-selective membranes, bio-physics and non-Newtonian hydrodynamics.
